// HOW TO PLAY

Each card has two letters on it.
PLAY — Click a card in your hand, then click an asteroid to add those letters to it.
WORD — Click any asteroid to spell a word from its letters and destroy it.
You can play cards and attempt words freely at any time — no turns, no waiting.

Each word must be longer than the last.
Every 3 words destroyed you advance a level — choose an upgrade and asteroids get faster.
Earth has 20 HP. Asteroids deal damage when they hit — bigger asteroids hurt more.
